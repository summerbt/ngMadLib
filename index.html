Mad Libs consist of a short story on each page with many key words replaced with blanks. Each blank is specified with a lexical or other category, such as "noun", "verb", "place", or "part of the body". You will contribute some word for the specified type for each blank. Finally, you will click to reveal the completed story. The result is usually comic, surreal and somewhat nonsensical.
